About Mobile Mutts

At Mobile Mutts, we are fully invested in the best possible care of your furry family! We provide energy releasing walks for more playful pups and relaxing walks for the easy going couch potatoes . We will provide him/her with all the physical activity customized to their individual needs.

Additionally, if you ever want pet sitting while you are away, we also offer in-home overnight care. Whether you want your furry friend to get some exercise on a run, enjoy some fresh air and a potty break on a walk, or receive some fun ball-time, we are here to assist with your pet service needs. Mobile Mutts is committed to improving your pet’s overall quality of life while you’re away.

We are a small, dedicated business that is passionate about dogs, and make it our business to be knowledgeable and current in all facets of pet care, including breed specific, so that you can rely on us when you can’t be there for your pet.

Our team goes the extra mile to establish a personal relationship with not only your pet, but you as well. With Mobile Mutts, you are more than just a client, we become part of your pack.

  • Overnight pet sitting for your dogs and/or cats
